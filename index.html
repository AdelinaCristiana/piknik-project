About us

Pik-Nik Restaurant

Restaurant Pik-Nik is a family business, built with love by people passionate about gastronomy. To give a perfect taste to the food we use organic ingredients from local producers, home-made products, we combine tastes and spices from all over the world, so that any gastronomic choice you make will be a complex and complete one. To receive an excellent steak, it is not enough for the restaurant's kitchen to be equipped with quality meat. It also matters a lot how it is cooked, by whom it is cooked, but also the oven / grill in which it is prepared. Excellent service, pleasant environment and very varied menu, very tasty dishes.
